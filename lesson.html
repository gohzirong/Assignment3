Lesson 1

Why is Covid-19 important?

The novel human coronavirus disease Covid-19 has become the fifth documented pandemic since the 1918 flu pandemic. The infection can be spread through coughs, sneezes, huffing and puffing, and likely even loud talking, according to Forthal. In addition, the virus also attacks the lower respiratory tract where it can lead to pneumonia. Covid-19 also has a high infection rate.

Lesson 2

Who are prone to Covid-19?

Everyone who has not taken any Covid-19 vaccine is susceptible to Covid-19. However the most susceptible people are:

1. Elderly

2. People with lung conditions

3. People with heart diseases or diabetes

4. People with weakened immune system

Lesson 3

Symptoms of Covid-19

Most common symptoms: Fever, Dry cough, Tiredness

Less common symptoms: Aches and pains, Sore throat, Diarrhoea

Serious symptoms: Difficulty breathing, Chest pain, Loss of speech

Lesson 4

Dealing with Covid-19

To prevent infection, do the following:

1. Wash your hands regularly with soap and water

2. Avoid touching your face

3. Stay home if you feel unwell

4. Refrain from smoking

5. Practice safe-distancing
